How to Buy a Fire Emblem Game

How to buy a physical copy of one of the Wii, GBA, or DS games

  1. Call ahead to your local Gamstop, ask if they have any Fire Emblem titles
    available (they often don't!) if they do have some titles avaliable: Go to Gamstop
  2. Look in the Wii, DS, or GBA section
  3. Review all their available FE titles
  4. Ask a Gamestop Employee if they have any other FE titles in storage
  5. Research the content of the FE game titles available to you
  6. Select the one you like best and purchase it!
  7. Take it home and enjoy it to your heart's content!!
What to do if you weren't able to find a title you wanted to purchase at Gamestop
  1. Ask an employee to order any title you desire
  2. Titles can be shipped to a Gamestop location in under a week
  3. Come back later and pick up your desired title!
